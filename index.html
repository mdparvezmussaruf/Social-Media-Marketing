Facebook Marketing

Mass reach across demographics with Meta’s Business Suite and cross‑placements (Instagram, Messenger, Audience Network).

Organic Playbook

  • Mix posts: images, Reels, Stories, Live, and carousels.
  • Post 3–5×/week; reply to comments & Messenger quickly.
  • Use Groups for community; Events for launches/webinars.
  • Pin top posts and add a clear Page CTA (e.g., Shop Now).

Ads & Targeting

Formats: Image, Video, Carousel, Collection/Shop, Reels, Lead Ads, Messenger.

Targeting: Core (age, geo, interests), Custom (site visitors, engagers), Lookalike (1–5%).

  • Install Meta Pixel + Conversions API.
  • Use Advantage+ Shopping for ecommerce.
  • Retarget 3/7/30‑day windows.
Analytics & KPIs
  • Reach/Impressions, Engagement rate, CTR.
  • Cost per Result, ROAS, Lead quality.
Pro Tips & Mistakes
  • Test 3–5 creatives per ad set; kill losers fast.
  • Avoid text‑heavy images; prioritize thumb‑stopping visuals.
  • Warm up with value posts before hard offers.
Posting Cadence
  • 4–7 posts/week
  • Reels priority
Optimization Ideas
  • A/B test headlines and creatives
  • Retarget 7/30‑day engagers
  • Iterate on top 10% posts

Instagram Marketing

Visual storytelling via Reels, Stories, and Grid. Great for lifestyle, fashion, beauty, travel, and food.

Organic Playbook

  • Prioritize Reels (9–30s) with strong hooks & captions.
  • Stories daily for behind‑the‑scenes, polls, and Q&A.
  • Grid carousels for education; cohesive brand aesthetic.
  • Use 3–8 relevant hashtags; hook in first line.

Ads & Targeting

Formats: Reels, Stories, Image, Video, Carousel, Shop/Collection.

Targeting: Core, Custom (IG engagers/profile), Lookalike.

  • Optimize for Add‑to‑Cart or Purchase after learning phase.
  • UGC‑style creatives often outperform polished ads.
Analytics & KPIs
  • Plays & Watch time
  • Saves/Shares
  • Profile visits & Follows
  • Outbound clicks
Pro Tips & Mistakes
  • Hook in first 2 seconds; on‑screen captions
  • Post 4–7×/week; batch create
  • Collabs & influencer whitelisting
Posting Cadence
  • 4–7 posts/week
  • Reels/Stories daily
Optimization Ideas
  • Test hooks & thumbnails
  • Saveable carousels
  • CTA to DM for offers

ℹ️ Connect Meta Pixel; track IG profile visitors and site actions.

TikTok Marketing

Short‑form video built on discovery and trends. High virality potential with creator‑driven culture.

Organic Playbook

  • Post 4–10×/week to find message–market fit.
  • Structure: HOOK (0–2s) → Value → CTA.
  • Native editing, fast cuts, captions.
  • Engage comments; stitch/duet relevant videos.

Ads & Targeting

Formats: In‑Feed, Spark Ads, TopView, Carousel, Live Shopping.

Targeting: Interests/behavior, Custom (engagers/site), Lookalike.

  • Boost best organic videos with Spark Ads.
  • Creator UGC variations for scale.
  • Optimize View Content → ATC → Purchase.
Analytics & KPIs
  • Views & Average watch time
  • Full‑watch rate & Shares
  • Profile visits
  • Conversions
Pro Tips & Mistakes
  • Vertical 9:16; subject fills frame
  • Cuts every 1–2s; add captions
  • Join trends only when on‑brand
Posting Cadence
  • 5–10 posts/week
  • Trend‑aligned content
Optimization Ideas
  • Refilm top hooks
  • Creator collabs
  • Comment CTAs

ℹ️ Install TikTok Pixel for conversions; use Events API as needed.

LinkedIn Marketing

B2B platform for decision‑makers: demand gen, ABM, thought leadership, and recruiting.

Organic Playbook

  • Founders/SMEs post POVs weekly; employees engage.
  • Mix: text posts, carousels (PDF), native video, docs.
  • Company Page + Showcase Pages for products.
  • Use Events + Live for webinars.

Ads & Targeting

Formats: Single Image, Document/Carousel, Video, Lead Gen Forms, Message/Conversation.

Targeting: Job title/seniority, Company size/industry, Skills, Groups, ABM lists.

  • Gate mid‑funnel content with Lead Gen Forms.
  • Install Insight Tag for website retargeting.
Analytics & KPIs
  • Impressions & Engagement rate
  • Follower growth
  • Lead form fill rate
  • Cost per Lead & Pipeline influenced
Pro Tips & Mistakes
  • Value > virality. Teach, don’t tease.
  • Use proof assets (case studies, benchmarks).
  • Remarket to site visitors & engagers.
Posting Cadence
  • 3–5 posts/week
  • 1 live/webinar per month
Optimization Ideas
  • Carousel frameworks
  • Employee advocacy
  • Lead Gen Forms + email nurture

ℹ️ Add LinkedIn Insight Tag to track conversions & audiences.

YouTube Marketing

Search + recommendations for long‑form education, reviews, and storytelling. Evergreen growth potential.

Organic Playbook

  • Map content to high‑intent keywords (use autosuggest).
  • Compelling title/thumbnail; 5–15s hook; chapters.
  • Use end screens & cards to increase session time.
  • Repurpose to Shorts with punchy hooks.

Ads & Targeting

Formats: Skippable In‑Stream, Non‑Skippable, In‑Feed (Discovery), Bumper, Shorts Ads.

Targeting: Custom intent (search terms), Affinity/In‑market, Remarketing.

  • Optimize for conversions with GA4 + Google tag.
  • Creative sequence: Problem → Proof → Product.
Analytics & KPIs
  • CTR (thumb/title)
  • Average view duration
  • Retention curve
  • Subs gained
  • Conversions
Pro Tips & Mistakes
  • Design thumbnails first; earn the click
  • Deliver on the title fast
  • Hook → Value → CTA; remove fluff
Posting Cadence
  • 1 video/week
  • 2–4 Shorts/week
Optimization Ideas
  • A/B test thumbnails
  • Chapters & end screens
  • CTA in first 60s

ℹ️ Connect GA4 & conversions; link YouTube to Google Ads.

Pinterest Marketing

Inspiration & planning with commercial intent (home, DIY, fashion, food, travel). Long content lifespan.

Organic Playbook

  • Create idea pins & standard pins with keyworded titles.
  • Fresh pins weekly; seasonal boards.
  • Use vertical 1000×1500 images with text overlays.
  • Link to blogs, collections, and lead magnets.

Ads & Targeting

Formats: Standard, Video, Shopping, Collections, Idea Ads.

Targeting: Interests/keywords, Actalike audiences, Retargeting.

  • Upload product feed for Shopping.
  • Optimize for Add‑to‑Cart and Checkout.
Analytics & KPIs
  • Saves
  • Outbound clicks
  • Engagements
  • Top pins
  • Conversions
Pro Tips & Mistakes
  • Text overlays to clarify value
  • Plan 6–8 weeks ahead for seasonality
  • Use Rich Pins for metadata
Posting Cadence
  • 5–10 pins/week
  • Seasonal boards
Optimization Ideas
  • Keyword‑rich titles
  • Fresh creative variations
  • Link to evergreen content

ℹ️ Add Pinterest tag; enable product catalog for Shopping ads.

X (Twitter) Marketing

Real‑time platform for news, customer support, and niche communities. Great for brands with active voices.

Organic Playbook

  • Post 1–3 short insights daily; reply to others.
  • Use threads for mini‑guides and breakdowns.
  • Host Spaces for AMAs; pin key offers.
  • Monitor brand mentions; respond fast.

Ads & Targeting

Formats: Promoted posts, Video, Carousel, Website clicks, Lead gen.

Targeting: Interests/topics, Keywords, Follower look‑alikes, Retargeting.

  • Short native videos (6–20s) perform well.
  • Align with trending topics carefully.
Analytics & KPIs
  • Impressions
  • Engagements
  • Profile visits
  • Link clicks
  • Follows
  • Conversions
Pro Tips & Mistakes
  • Clarity over cleverness; deliver value
  • Use charts/images to stand out
  • Join relevant lists to expand reach
Posting Cadence
  • Daily posts
  • Weekly thread or Space
Optimization Ideas
  • A/B test hooks
  • Reply strategy to larger accounts
  • Pin high‑performing tweet
